# Learning Markdown

## Markdown

is a way to style text on the web. You control the display of the document; formatting words as bold or italic and more.

## Anything without order becomes incomprehensible, so we will summarize the most important basics of this language 

### Heading

To create a heading, add one to six **#** symbols before your heading text. The number of # you use will determine the size of the heading.

## Text

**Style** | **Syntax**
--------- | ----------
**Blode** | ** ** or __ __
*italic*  | * * or _ _
~~Strikethrough~~ | ~~ ~~
***All blode and italic*** | *** ***

## Links
You can create an inline link by wrapping link text in brackets [ ], and then wrapping the URL in parentheses ( ).
