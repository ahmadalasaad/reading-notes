## JavaScript has the following types of operators:

* **Assignment operators**

x = y , x = x + y , x = x - y , x = x * y , x = x / y ,.... 

* **Comparison operators**

x == y , x < y , x > y , x >= y , x <= y  

* **Arithmetic operators**

x % y , x++ , x-- , ...

* **Bitwise operators**

x&y ,x|y , ~x , ....

* **Logical operators**

x&&y , x||y , !X

* **String operators**

'x ' + 'y'

* **Conditional (ternary) operator**
